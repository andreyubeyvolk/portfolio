Stickerburg

[x]

Stickerburg is a print studio specializing in stickers, with a strong craft-driven reputation and a long-standing identity. Over time, the logo stopped reflecting the level of expertise and maturity the studio had reached.

The goal was to evolve it into something more systematic and considered, while keeping it simple, recognizable, and true to its character.

We approached Saint Petersburg as a structural language rather than a set of symbols. Angled building corners, cast-iron forms, and urban geometry informed the visual logic.

The identity centers on a refined multi-sided form, somewhere between an architectural fragment and a stamped sign. Paired with restrained typography, it builds a system that feels calm, precise, and repeatable.
